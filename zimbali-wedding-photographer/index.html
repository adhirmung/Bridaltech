North Coast Luxury

Zimbali is spectacular. Your photographs should match.

The Fairmont Zimbali Resort sits on the KZN North Coast — a five-star beachfront property where the Indian Ocean forms the backdrop to some of South Africa's most breathtaking weddings. The lush coastal forest, the ocean views, the architectural grandeur of the resort itself — all of it creates an extraordinary photographic canvas.

"The photos were not posed and routine, they were natural, capturing special moments — every single one."
— Reena Desai, Fairmont Zimbali Resort

Adhir has photographed destination weddings at Zimbali from couples based in Australia, the UK, and across South Africa. He understands the venue's spaces — from the grand ballroom to the beachfront ceremony area — and knows exactly how to use the coastal light to create images that feel timeless.

Indian and multicultural weddings at Zimbali are a speciality. The resort is a favourite for Hindu, Tamil, and Muslim families hosting grand celebrations on the North Coast.
